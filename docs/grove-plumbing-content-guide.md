# Grove Plumbing Redesign Demo — Content Guide

## Canadian English rules
Use Canadian spellings throughout:
- colour
- neighbour
- centre
- licence (noun) / licensed (verb/adjective context is fine)
- metre
- favourite
- honour
- organisation

Do not use American spelling unless it appears in a brand or source asset.

## Tone
Write in a way that feels:
- local
- straightforward
- confident
- friendly
- trustworthy

Avoid:
- inflated claims
- cheesy sales language
- robotic copy
- jargon-heavy technical writing
- generic filler like “we are committed to excellence”

## Writing style
- Clear headlines
- Short paragraphs
- Strong scannability
- Specific benefits
- Direct calls to action

## Core homepage headline options
Use one of these styles:
- Plumbing, Heating & Gas Done Right
- Local Plumbing, Heating & Gas Services You Can Count On
- Trusted Plumbing, Heating & Gas Services for South Delta Homes

## Supporting subheadline direction
Mention:
- local service area
- trust
- convenience
- request/booking ease

Example:
Serving Ladner, Tsawwassen, and North Delta with dependable plumbing, heating, gas, and drain services — with a modern, easy way to request service.

## Homepage section order
1. Hero
2. Trust bar
3. Services overview
4. Why choose Grove
5. Recent work / real project gallery
6. Booking preview
7. Testimonials
8. Service areas
9. FAQs
10. Final CTA

## Service page structure
Each service page should include:
- strong H1
- short intro
- common problems section
- what’s included
- why homeowners choose Grove
- FAQs
- CTA section

## Suggested service pages
### 1. Plumbing Services
General residential plumbing repairs, replacements, and installations.

### 2. Drain Cleaning
Drain clogs, slow drains, backups, maintenance.

### 3. Water Heater Repair & Installation
Hot water tank repair/replacement, troubleshooting, upgrades.

### 4. Heating Services
Home heating system support, maintenance, and repairs.

### 5. Gas Fitting
Safe, licensed gas line and appliance connection work.

### 6. Emergency Plumbing
Urgent leaks, bursts, drainage issues, no-hot-water situations.

## Service area page structure
Each area page should:
- name the city in the H1
- mention local service
- list relevant services
- add trust/reassurance
- include CTA
- include FAQs
- mention nearby communities naturally where appropriate

## Suggested recurring trust statements
Use variations of:
- Local service you can rely on
- Practical solutions without the runaround
- Licensed and insured work
- Red Seal certified experience
- Friendly, professional service for homeowners in Ladner, Tsawwassen, and North Delta

## Booking page copy direction
Do not make it sound like guaranteed instant booking if no backend exists.

Use language like:
- Request service online
- Choose your preferred date and time
- We’ll follow up to confirm your appointment
- Tell us a bit about the problem and we’ll take it from there

## Testimonial direction
Testimonials should sound specific and local.

Good style:
- mentions the problem
- mentions responsiveness or professionalism
- sounds like a real homeowner
- avoids obvious fake-review language

## FAQ examples
### Home page FAQ ideas
- What areas do you serve?
- Do you offer emergency plumbing services?
- Can I request service online?
- Do you handle heating and gas work too?
- Are you licensed and insured?

### Service page FAQ ideas
- How quickly can you come out?
- Do you provide estimates?
- What brands or systems do you work with?
- Can you help with older homes?
- What should I do before you arrive?

## Contact page direction
Include:
- phone number
- email
- service areas
- map section
- request service CTA

## Image usage guidance
Use real Grove images wherever they strengthen trust:
- van for hero/supporting sections
- job photos for service credibility
- family/team photo for About

Use generic supporting imagery sparingly and only if needed.

## Final test
Before approving any page, ask:
- Does this feel local?
- Does this feel believable?
- Does this make Grove look established?
- Would a homeowner know what to do next in under 5 seconds?
